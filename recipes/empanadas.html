Recipe of empanadas

A classic Argentinian food, this is a recipe whose filling can vary but in this case we will use a meat filling.

Ingredients

  • 500gr of minced beef
  • 1/2 kilogram chopped onion
  • 2 green onions, chopped
  • 1 clove minced garlic
  • 2 hard-boiled eggs, chopped
  • 3 tablespoons cornstarch Cornstarch
  • 1 can of tomato
  • oil required amount
  • dry ground chili to taste
  • cumin powder to taste
  • paprika powder to taste
  • 12 empanada covers
  • 1 egg to paint

Instructions

  1. In a saucepan, sauté the onion, green onion and garlic.
  2. Once the vegetables are well cooked, add the minced meat along with the canned tomato. Cook for approximately 10 minutes.
  3. Add the Cornstarch dissolved in the cold water and cook for 1 more minute. Season with the spices and the hard-boiled egg. Reserve and cool.
  4. Assemble the empanadas, cover them and paint with the beaten egg.
  5. Cook in the oven at 200°C until golden brown.
  6. Enjoy!
