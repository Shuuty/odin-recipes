Recipe of vitel toné

Vitel toné is a classic at Argentine Christmas. It is a delicious dish, very easy to prepare and ideal for our summer parties, a time for fresh foods.

Ingredients

  • One Eye Round of 1.5kg approx.
  • Two glasses of white wine
  • Two meat broth
  • Two bay leaf
  • Two can of tuna
  • Two hard-boiled egg yolks
  • Two tbsp. Of olive oil
  • 3-4 anchovies
  • One tbsp. of vinegar
  • Juice of half a lemon
  • 1 tbsp. mustard
  • 2-3 tbsp. mayonnaise
  • 1 tbsp. capers + extra capers to decorate
    •

    Instructions

    1. Marinate the Eye Round in the 2 glasses of wine overnight.
    2. After this time, bridle the eye round (tie it, so that it retains its round shape)
    3. Boil it in the broth for 1 hour in a common pot or 40 minutes in a pressure cooker.
    4. Also let it cool in the broth.
    5. Meanwhile, put 1 large ladle of the broth in which the eye round was made into the blender along with the 2 hard-boiled egg yolks, the tuna, the anchovies, the 2 tbsp. of olive oil, the tbsp. of vinegar, lemon juice, mustard, mayonnaise and tbsp. of capers. Blend all the ingredients until you obtain a fine cream.
    6. Cut the eye round, as thin as possible.
    7. Arrange on a platter and cover with the tuna and anchovy sauce. Throw more capers on top.
